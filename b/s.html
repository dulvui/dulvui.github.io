Personalized url shortener s9i.cc

24.06.2022

Why?

Short URLs are important today. They are easier to remember and share. Thats why I use my own s9i.cc and s3i.cc domains to have short and sexy links. This site for example becomes s9i.cc/b/s
I chose this URL because it's like the well known i18n or l10n. My domain is simondalvai.com and the numbers of letters between the starting "s" and ending "i" is 9.

How?

I simply bought this short domains on porkbun.com for only $8.55/year. Then I used the porkbun builtin URL forwarding with the "Include the requested URI path in the redirection" checked.
